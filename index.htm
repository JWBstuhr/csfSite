My Pets
Project

I have three pets, all of them cats. They're all pretty unique in terms of personality, I think.
I think many pet owners feel that way, though, due to familiarity.
I'm going to describe them here on this site.
(I'm terrible at good transitions.)

Squat


She's my favorite cat of the three. Some unique identifying traits:

  • Very loud. Doesn't like to be shut in the front of the house. If she is, she will meow. Loudly.
  • She will mush and lay on you if you don't show any signs of getting up soon.
  • Likes to stare at you.

She's named Squat because we intended to only let her stay for winter, but now she's lived with us for something like 4 years.

Reeses


Reeses is the strangest of the bunch. She's the first cat we brought in from outside, and she does many weird things.

  • She likes to meow way more than other cats, and will "converse" with you.
  • She actively points her laser pointer at things and people.
  • She licks guitar strings sometimes, just to make the noise.

She's considered the science cat by most, and is named for her fur colors.

Smokey


This one's not really my cat, more my mom's. I'm not all too familiar with him, for reasons that will become apparently in his list:

  • He's quiet. Very quiet. His meows are inaudible most of the time.
  • He spends most of his time lying in his cat bed.
  • He really doesn't like strangers.

He's just the shy cat I don't see much of. He was presumably named because of his fur color, but I can't confirm as he was taken into our care from a friend of my mom.
